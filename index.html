Dent-Free Perfection

Paintless Dent Repair

Advanced PDR techniques that remove dents without affecting your vehicle's original paint finish.

Paintless Dent Repair (PDR) is the most cost-effective and paint-preserving solution for dents and dings. Using specialized tools, our technicians carefully massage the metal back to its original shape — no fillers, no repainting, and no risk to your vehicle's factory finish.

PDR is ideal for:

  • Hail damage — including large hail storm damage across multiple panels
  • Door dings from parking lots
  • Minor creases and round dents where the paint is intact
  • Preserving your vehicle's original paint, which helps maintain resale value

PDR is faster and significantly more affordable than traditional dent repair. Most jobs can be completed same-day, and the results are indistinguishable from the original factory panel.
